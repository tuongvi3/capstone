ARCHIVE OF ASIAN MEMES

There’s a space on the internet that are filled with struggles of growing up with strict Asian parents, language puns, Western teachers not being able to pronounce your name and depictions of Asians’ obsession with K-pop and boba.These memes capture what it’s like to grow up as a young asian. Asian memes can be found on social media platforms such as Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, and other similar online platforms to seek entertainment by viewing funny memes.

Asian memes touch on topics of the pressure of unrealistic expectations of success from your parents, Asian food, what it’s like being a minority in their home countries, and much more. They mainly focus on Asian culture as experienced by the children of migrants.

Meme groups like the Facebook group Subtle Asian Traits are dedicated to internet memes, jokes and discussions surrounding the Asian American experience. Subtle Asian Traits has grown quickly, with over a million followers since it started in December 2018.

These meme groups give young Asians a voice and to reflect on their experiences. The viewing of memes can be a way to relieve their experiences and laugh at them.

This website will be an archive of Asian memes, click through the 6 different categories to see which one’s you can relate with, that makes you laugh, or you just find entertaining.
